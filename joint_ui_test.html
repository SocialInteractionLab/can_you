Joint probability UI — quick prototype

Imagine 100 random people are given the following situation:

You're at a dinner party and someone pulls out a scrambled Rubik's cube. It gets passed around and eventually lands in your hands. They look at you and say:

"Can you solve the Rubik's cube?"

Drag the crosshair to show how many of the 100 people fall into each group.
